Can you change the low battery warning on Mac?

macOS does not give most users a simple setting to change exactly when the low battery warning appears. BatteryCountdown gives you a clearer workaround: choose when the countdown starts and see remaining time before shutdown in the menu bar.

Low battery warning Mac FAQ

Does BatteryCountdown replace the macOS low battery warning?

No. It adds a clearer menu bar countdown so you can act before the Mac shuts down.

Can I change battery warning timing on Mac?

BatteryCountdown lets you choose when its own countdown starts, from 1% to 20% battery.

Does it work while charging?

Yes. When charging, the dropdown can show time remaining until fully charged.
